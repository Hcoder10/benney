## Itinerary rules
1. Exactly 30 activity IDs per family, in slot order (Day 1 slot 0..5, Day 2 slot 0..5, ...).
2. Realistic chain — breakfast slot should be a cafe/restaurant, evening slot a dinner spot, late-morning/lunch-afternoon are flexible.
3. Don't put a winery + fine-dining + late bar in a slot the family can't reach (luxury budget can; shoestring can't).
4. Geographic continuity within a day — if morning is in SF, don't put afternoon in Big Basin.
5. Family with kid_ages != "none": don't use activities with kid_ok=false.
6. mobility=limited/wheelchair: skip mobility_ok=false activities.
7. budget_tier=shoestring: avoid premium/luxury activities unless the family is splurging once.
8. The 50k cohort will tally what families "like this" picked — your job is to produce **diverse but coherent** trips so the population stats are meaningful, not 50 identical schedules.
